01.About me

Hello! My name is Facundo and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started in 2020 when I tried to make a blog by myself with HTML & CSS. Then I realized I could join two of my passions, programming websites and bringing them to life via design.

I love to work with JavaScript technologies. Currently, im focused on learning everything that is related to Web Development. I'm also passionate about exploring new technologies that I can use to solve real-life problems.
